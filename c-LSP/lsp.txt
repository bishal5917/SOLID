
LISKOV SUBSTITUITION PRINCIPLE

IT states that the object (such as a class) and a subobject (such as a class that extends other class) must 
be interchangeable without breaking the program.

LSP states that the objects of a superclass should be replacable with the objects of its subclasses without breaking the 
application.In other words,we want the object of our subclasses beahaving the same way as the objects of our superclass.
